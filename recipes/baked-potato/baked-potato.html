Perfect Baked Potato

This recipe provides a method for baking a perfect potato with a crispy skin and fluffy interior.

Ingredients:

  • Russet Potatoes: Ideal for baking due to their starchy nature.
  • Olive Oil: To coat the potatoes, helping to crisp the skin.
  • Salt: Enhances flavor and aids in creating a crispy skin.

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Potatoes: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Scrub the potatoes clean and dry them thoroughly.
  2. Coat and Season: Rub each potato with olive oil and sprinkle generously with salt.
  3. Bake: Place the potatoes directly on the middle rack of the oven. Bake for 1 hour, or until the skins are crisp and a fork easily pierces the flesh.
  4. Serve: Allow the potatoes to cool slightly, then cut a slit in the top and fluff the insides with a fork. Add your favorite toppings.

Summary:

This recipe involves cleaning and drying russet potatoes, coating them with olive oil and salt, then baking them at 350°F for an hour. The result is a baked potato with a perfectly crispy skin and fluffy interior, ready for your favorite toppings.
