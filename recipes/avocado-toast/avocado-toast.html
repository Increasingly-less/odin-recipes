Meyer Lemon Avocado Toast

This recipe provides a refreshing and flavorful twist on the classic avocado toast, featuring Meyer lemon for an added zest.

Ingredients:

  • Avocado: Ripe and mashed.
  • Meyer Lemon: Juice and zest for a bright, citrusy flavor.
  • Bread: Toasted slices of your choice.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes for added spice.
  • Olive Oil: For drizzling.

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Avocado: Mash the avocado in a bowl. Add Meyer lemon juice, zest, salt, and pepper to taste.
  2. Toast the Bread: Toast slices of your favorite bread until golden brown.
  3. Assemble the Toast: Spread the avocado mixture onto the toasted bread slices.
  4. Finish and Serve: Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with red pepper flakes. Serve immediately.
