Chicken Tikka Masala

This recipe offers a delicious way to prepare Chicken Tikka Masala, a popular Indian dish featuring marinated chicken in a spiced tomato cream sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs.
  • Yogurt and Spices: For marinating the chicken (yogurt, lemon juice, ginger, garlic, garam masala, chili powder, cumin).
  • Sauce: Tomato sauce, cream, butter, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices (garam masala, cumin, paprika, salt).

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix yogurt, lemon juice, and spices. Add chicken, coat well, and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Grill or bake the marinated chicken until fully cooked.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In a large pan, saut√© garlic and ginger in butter. Add tomato sauce and spices, simmer, then stir in cream.
  4. Combine and Simmer: Add cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for a few minutes until flavors meld together.

Summary:

This recipe involves marinating chicken in a spiced yogurt mixture, cooking the chicken, and then simmering it in a rich, creamy tomato sauce. The result is a flavorful and aromatic Chicken Tikka Masala, perfect for serving with rice or naan.
