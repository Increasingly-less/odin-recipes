Chicken Noodle Soup

This recipe provides a simple and fast way to make a comforting bowl of chicken noodle soup.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Broth: Provides the base for the soup.
  • Chicken Breast: Cooked and shredded.
  • Carrots, Celery, and Onion: Classic vegetables for flavor and nutrition.
  • Egg Noodles: The noodles for the soup.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, and optional additions like parsley for flavor.

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Vegetables: In a large pot, saut√© chopped onion, carrots, and celery until tender.
  2. Add Broth and Chicken: Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil. Add shredded chicken.
  3. Cook Noodles: Stir in egg noodles and cook until tender.
  4. Season: Add salt, pepper, and any other desired seasonings to taste.
